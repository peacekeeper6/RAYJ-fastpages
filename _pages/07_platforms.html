Spotify

  1. Spotify is a Swedish audio streaming platform and was founded on founded on April 23, 2006 by its creators, Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon. As of 2022, Spotify has 433 million active users monthly, listening to the millions of songs and podcasts on its database.
